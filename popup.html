Flashing Light Protection Settings

About This Extension

This extension helps protect users from flashing lights in videos by detecting rapid changes in brightness and applying a blackout filter to the video. You can adjust the detection sensitivity and dimming level, or choose to turn the video completely black when flashing lights are detected.

Settings:

  • Detection Sensitivity (Threshold): Adjust the sensitivity for detecting flashing lights. A lower value means higher sensitivity.
  • Dimming Level (%): Set the level of dimming applied to the video when flashing lights are detected.
  • Turn Video to Black: Enable this option to turn the video completely black when flashing lights are detected.
